Hi there!
My name is Lasya Priya.
I have always been captivated by the rich cultural heritage of India, and my traveling interests lie in exploring the magnificent temples that dot this diverse country. India's temples are not only architectural marvels but also significant spiritual and cultural landmarks. From the ancient and awe-inspiring temples of Varanasi to the intricate carvings of the temples in Khajuraho, each visit fills me with a sense of awe and tranquility.

The intricate designs, vibrant colors, and spiritual ambiance create a truly immersive experience. I find solace in seeking blessings at these sacred sites and learning about the fascinating mythological stories associated with them. The spiritual journey through India's temples allows me to connect with the country's deep-rooted traditions and understand the profound impact of religion on its people.


In addition to exploring temples, I am also drawn to the breathtaking hill stations and picturesque beaches that India has to offer. The serene and tranquil hill stations provide a refreshing escape from the bustling cities.
From the misty hills of Shimla and the lush tea plantations of Munnar to the majestic peaks of the Himalayas in Manali, these hill stations offer a serene environment, breathtaking landscapes, and opportunities for adventurous activities like trekking and paragliding. On the other hand, India's stunning coastline boasts some of the most beautiful beaches in the world.

DESTINATIONS

Temples

Temples provide a sense of community and unity among believers, fostering a shared connection and a place for religious ceremonies, rituals, and celebrations. These architectural marvels, adorned with intricate carvings, vibrant paintings, and ornate sculptures, often serve as a visual representation of the beliefs and mythology of the respective faith

Beaches

Beaches are beautiful coastal areas where land meets the sea or ocean, typically characterized by the presence of sand or pebbles. They are popular destinations for relaxation, recreation, and enjoying the natural beauty of the shoreline. Beaches offer opportunities for activities like swimming, sunbathing, beach volleyball, surfing, snorkeling, and building sandcastles. They provide a refreshing escape from the bustling city life, allowing people to unwind and connect with nature.

Hill Stations

Hill stations are picturesque mountainous destinations that offer respite from the heat and hustle of the plains. Located at higher altitudes, these towns or settlements are characterized by cool and pleasant weather, lush greenery, and panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys. Hill stations are popular tourist destinations.

8th JUNE / YADAGIRI GUTTA NARASIMHA SWAMI TEMPLE, YADAGIRI

Yadagirigutta Temple, also known as Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple, is a famous Hindu pilgrimage site located in Yadagirigutta, a small town in the Nalgonda district of Telangana, India. The temple is dedicated to Lord Narasimha, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu, who is worshipped as the presiding deity.

16th MARCH GOA

Goa is a coastal state located on the western coast of India, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese influence, and rich cultural heritage. It is a popular tourist destination, attracting both domestic and international travelers.

10th JANUARY / SRISAILAM DAM SRISAILAM

Srisailam is a prominent pilgrimage town located in the Kurnool district of Andhra Pradesh, India. It is nestled amidst the Nallamala Hills on the banks of the Krishna River and is considered one of the twelve Jyotirlinga temples dedicated to Lord Shiva
