Shree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Kerala

The Shree Padmanabhaswamy Temple is a Hindu temple located in Thiruvananthapuram, the capital of the state of Kerala, India. It is widely considered as the world's richest Hindu temple.The name of the city of 'Thiruvananthapuram' in Malayalam and Tamil translates to "The City of Ananta". The principal deity is Padmanabhaswamy (Vishnu), who is enshrined in the "Anantha Shayana" posture, the eternal yogic sleep on the infinite serpent Adi Shesha.Padmanabhaswamy is the tutelary deity of the Travancore royal family. The titular Maharaja of Travancore, Moolam Thirunal Rama Varma, is the current trustee of the temple.

Puri Jagannath Temple, Odisha

Puri Jagannath Temple, located in the coastal town of Puri in the state of Odisha, India, is one of the most revered and ancient Hindu temples in the country. It is dedicated to Lord Jagannath, a form of Lord Vishnu, along with his siblings Balabhadra and Subhadra. The most famous festival associated with Puri Jagannath Temple is the Rath Yatra, also known as the Chariot Festival, held annually. During this grand event, the deities are ceremoniously placed on lavishly decorated chariots and pulled by thousands of devotees through the streets of Puri. The Rath Yatra attracts a massive crowd of pilgrims who come to witness the divine procession and seek the blessings of Lord Jagannath. Puri Jagannath Temple is not only a center of religious devotion but also an emblem of Odisha's rich heritage, spirituality, and deep-rooted traditions.

Murudeshwar Temple, Karnataka

Murdeshwar is a town in Uttara Kannada district in the state of Karnataka, India, It is famous for the world's second tallest Shiva statue, the town lies on the coast of the Arabian Sea and is also famous for the Murudeshwara Temple.
Murudeshwar Temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the main places to visit in Murudeshwar. The town was earlier known as 'Mrideshwara' and later renamed as Murudeshwar after the construction of the temple. The main attraction of the temple is the enormous statue of Lord Shiva, known as the "Shiva Statue at Murudeshwar," which stands at a height of around 123 feet (37 meters). This magnificent statue is one of the tallest statues of Lord Shiva in the world and has become an iconic landmark of the region. The temple complex also includes other shrines, such as the Rameshwara Linga and the Murudeshwar Linga, which are considered sacred by devotees. The architecture of the temple showcases intricate carvings and beautiful sculptures, depicting various mythological stories and characters.
